Washers

Washers are influential, well-known individuals of sound character who are willing to demonstrate humility in a deeply personal way.

The washing of feet is a physical act involving close contact with a stranger. The washer lowers their head, gathers water, and gently washes the feet of another person — while engaging with them directly. This simple yet profound action requires quiet courage and humility.
