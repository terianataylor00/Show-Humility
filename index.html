Show
Humility

A global initiative with a single focus:

“Once a year, the world’s most powerful people wash the feet of those they serve.”

We believe there is no more powerful act to demonstrate humility. In a time when societies are fragmenting and trust is under strain, this simple, symbolic gesture shows that leaders care — and are willing to serve.

Why the
washing of
feet?
Who will the
washers be?

By “the most powerful people,” we mean the Presidents, Prime Ministers, and Sovereigns of all 206 states across the globe.

But leadership goes beyond politics. We also call upon influential figures from across society — including:

Government & Members of Parliament
Royalty, Lords, Military
Business & Faith Leaders
Media, Sports, & Cultural Icons

Each event is expected to have at least 70 washers each washing over 20 peoples feet, so at least a 1,400 are washed at every event.

Register Your State or City

We encourage every nation and city to create their own Show Humility movement and register it here on our website. We will do everything we can to support you.

Registration is simple. To begin, you only need:

  • An exclusive region (your nation or city)
  • A venue for the event
  • 12 committed “washers” to start with

With these foundations in place, you can launch a movement that inspires humility and unity in your community.
